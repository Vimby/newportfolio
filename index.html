About Me

I am a passionate self-taught developer with a diverse background in front-end and back-end development. My journey into the world of programming began when I received the Google Africa Certification Scholarship in partnership with Andela and Pluralsight, which ignited my enthusiasm for technology. From there, I dove deeper into web development through a comprehensive boot camp on Udemy and gained practical experience during the HNG6 internship. To further hone my skills, I completed a Full Stack Web Development certification program offered by ALX in collaboration with Udacity, equipping me with a solid foundation in both front-end and server-side technologies. Currently, I am expanding my expertise through SheCodes, where I have achieved advanced web development and AI certifications. I am actively freelancing, providing high-quality, responsive, mobile-friendly web applications to clients. I am open to full-time / remote opportunities where I can contribute my skills and grow as a developer.
